Crystal Collector!

The computer will generate a random number at the start of the game--this is the Computer Score.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your Player Score.


By matching your score to the randomized Computer Score, you win the game.
If you fail to match the computer score and your score surpasses it, you will lose the game. Think carefully!


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


The crystal values and the Computer Score will reset if you lose.
If you win, you can try your luck against new random Computer Scores with your winning crystal set.


Good luck!
