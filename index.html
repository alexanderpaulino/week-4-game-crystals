Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the randomized computer score.
If you fail to match the computer score and your score surpasses it, you will lose the game! Think carefully!


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal, so plan accordingly. Good luck!
